Vegan pancakes

Description

Wondrously Fluffy Vegan Pancakes, incredibly easy to make with a handful of pantry staples! Made in 1 bowl.

I love having a few staple recipes for when I'm craving baked sweet goods. If you have some basic baking ingredients around the house, make some pancakes! Ingredients

  • 75 g buckwheat flour
  • 125 ml rice drink
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, add the rice drink, sugar, oil and lime juice. Whisk until well combined.
  2. Add the flour to the wet ingredients, then sprinkle the baking powder and salt on top of the flour. Mix until just combined with a wooden spoon.
  3. Heat a large griddle or pan over medium-high heat. Drop about 1/3 cup of the batter onto the griddle. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown until the other side, about 1-2 minutes. Repeat with all the remaining batter.
  4. Serve with vegan butter, powdered sugar and/or maple syrup. The recipe can be easily doubled, tripled or quadrupled.
